# Literacy Guild Progress Model

## Role of DIBELS

DIBELS is used as a private progress check and teacher planning tool. It does not become a public score, leaderboard, or character level.

## Path connections

Voice connects most directly to oral reading fluency evidence.

Insight connects to comprehension evidence, including Maze-style checks and classroom tasks.

Wordcraft needs classroom-created vocabulary evidence because vocabulary growth is broader than a single progress check.

## Student-facing framing

Progress checks help answer:

- What is getting stronger?
- What still needs training?
- What strategy should I use next?

## Teacher-facing framing

Progress data informs grouping, teacher-table instruction, conferences, and next goals.

## Main rule

Assessment informs the game. It is not the game.
