# Student Quest Map

## Purpose

The Quest Map shows your route through the Literacy Guild.

You will have choices, but some quests are required. Everyone eventually completes the required destinations. You may move through some parts of the map in a different order.

## Main rule

You choose the route. The Game Master controls the map.

Choice does not mean skipping a skill forever. It means you get some control over how you move through the campaign.

## Quest types

| Type | What it means |
|---|---|
| Core Quest | Required skill quest. Everyone completes it eventually. |
| Choice Quest | Choose from options that train the same skill. |
| Side Quest | Optional extra practice, XP, title, or ability evidence. |
| Recovery Quest | Extra training route when a skill needs more practice. |
| Boss Quest | Larger proof of skill after training. |
| Portfolio Quest | Evidence review and reflection. |

## How to mark progress

Use these marks:

| Mark | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Not started | I have not attempted this quest yet. |
| Training | I am practising the skill. |
| Evidence ready | I have evidence to submit or check. |
| Complete | The Game Master has accepted the evidence. |
| Reroute | I need more practice or a different support. |
