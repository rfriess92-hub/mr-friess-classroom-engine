## Progress check reflection

After a private progress check, answer:

1. What is getting stronger?
2. What evidence shows that?
3. What still needs training?
4. What strategy should I use next?

## Weekly reflection

Choose one prompt:

- This week I improved at...
- A strategy that helped me was...
- One piece of evidence I am proud of is...
- Next week I need to train...
