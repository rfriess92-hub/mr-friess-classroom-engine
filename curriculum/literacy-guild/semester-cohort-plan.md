# Semester Cohort Implementation Plan

## Purpose

This version is for a shorter semester course with returning students.

## Launch stance

The teacher should name the shift directly. This version is more structured, more routine-based, and more focused on visible growth than the previous teaching style.

## Reset message

Last year used one style. This semester will feel different. We will use a clearer training model built around Voice, Insight, and Wordcraft. The goal is to see what skill is being trained, what evidence shows growth, and what the next move is.

## Campaign map

### Week 1: Reset and Baseline

- explain the change
- introduce three paths
- teach the daily routine
- collect private progress information

### Weeks 2-3: Voice Focus

- repeated reading
- phrasing
- accuracy
- personal growth reflection

### Weeks 4-5: Wordcraft Focus

- context clues
- word parts
- academic word use

### Weeks 6-7: Insight Focus

- gist
- evidence
- inference
- comprehension repair

### Weeks 8-10: Integrated Quests

- weekly quests using all three paths
- shorter boss tasks
- private conferences

### Weeks 11-14: Growth Campaign

- targeted practice by need
- stronger independent evidence
- party challenges where useful

### Final Weeks: Evidence and Reflection

- final growth snapshot
- portfolio review
- next-step planning

## Design note

This version should use less lore, fewer mechanics, and more direct explanation.
