# First Mini Quest: The Locked Notice

## Purpose

This is the first playable Literacy Guild quest packet.

It is designed for early launch, after students have been introduced to Voice, Insight, Wordcraft, the Guild Passport, and basic evidence logging.

The quest uses controlled choice, but it is not wide open. Students choose a route through the task while still producing required solo evidence.

## Quest overview

- Arc: Enter the Guild / Three-Path Training
- Quest type: Boss / Mini Quest
- Main paths: Voice, Insight, Wordcraft
- Recommended timing: one 79-minute class or two shorter classes
- Student product: one solo evidence response
- Teacher check: evidence shown / not yet / reroute

## Student-facing hook

A notice has been found on the Guild door. Some words are unclear, and the message needs to be read carefully before the Guild can move forward.

Your party can help you train, but your final evidence must be your own.

## Learning target

Students will be able to use at least two reading strategies to understand a short text and submit one clear piece of solo evidence.

## Ability cards used

Use only these three abilities:

| Path | Ability | Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| Voice | Echo Cast | Repeat a model read to build fluency. |
| Insight | Gist Map | Say what a section is mostly about. |
| Wordcraft | Context Torch | Use nearby words to infer meaning. |

## Party roles used

Use only these roles:

| Role | Job |
|---|---|
| Quest Captain | Keeps the group on task. |
| Insight Keeper | Checks gist and meaning. |
| Wordsmith | Tracks key words and context clues. |

## Quest text

### The Locked Notice

The notice was taped to the library door before anyone arrived.

It said:

The archive will open when the Guild proves it can read with care. Do not rush. A fast answer may miss the message. Read once to enter. Read again to understand. Find the words that matter. Use evidence before you decide.

At the bottom, someone had written one more line:

The first key is not hidden. It is in the way you read.

## Teacher read-aloud option

Read the text aloud once while students follow.

Then model one sentence again using Echo Cast:

Read once to enter. Read again to understand.

Keep the model calm and brief. This is not performance reading.

## Student route choices

Students choose one route.

| Route | Path focus | Required evidence |
|---|---|---|
| Route A | Voice + Insight | Reread reflection plus gist statement |
| Route B | Insight + Wordcraft | Gist statement plus context clue explanation |
| Route C | Voice + Wordcraft | Reread reflection plus context clue explanation |

All routes require solo evidence.

## Required training tasks

Before solo evidence, every student practises all three moves briefly.

### Voice: Echo Cast

Practise these lines:

Read once to enter. Read again to understand.

Student note:

- What sounded smoother after rereading?

### Insight: Gist Map

Answer:

- What is the notice mostly telling the Guild to do?

### Wordcraft: Context Torch

Choose one word:

- archive
- proves
- evidence

Answer:

- What do you think the word means here?
- Which nearby words helped you?

## 79-minute class flow

| Time | Segment | Teacher move | Student work |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 0-7 | Daily Encounter | Show the line: Read again to understand. | Predict what the line means. |
| 7-20 | Guild Lesson | Launch quest and model Echo Cast, Gist Map, Context Torch. | Practise each strategy briefly. |
| 20-40 | Party Training | Assign three party roles. | Practise route options with party support. |
| 40-55 | Route Choice | Confirm each student's chosen route. | Choose Route A, B, or C. |
| 55-68 | Solo Evidence | Protect quiet work time. | Complete solo evidence. |
| 68-75 | Game Master Check | Quick scan for evidence shown / not yet / reroute. | Submit or revise. |
| 75-79 | Character Log | Award simple XP and name next step. | Log XP, evidence, and next move. |

## XP guidance

Keep XP simple.

| Action | XP |
|---|---:|
| Used an ability card correctly | 15 |
| Completed party role responsibly | 10 |
| Submitted solo evidence | 20 |
| Revised after feedback | 15 |
| Completed Character Log reflection | 10 |

Do not award all categories to every student. Choose what fits the evidence.

## Teacher evidence check

Use this quick check.

| Check | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Evidence shown | Student used the strategy and produced understandable evidence. |
| Not yet | Student attempted the task, but evidence is unclear or incomplete. |
| Reroute | Student needs a support route before the quest is complete. |

## Reroute options

Use one of these if needed:

| Need | Reroute |
|---|---|
| Student cannot state gist | Reread the notice with teacher table and use a sentence frame. |
| Student cannot explain word meaning | Use Context Torch with a different word and nearby clue. |
| Student avoids rereading | Use choral read or echo read privately with a partner or teacher. |
| Student writes too little | Oral rehearsal before writing. |

## Sentence frames

Use only if needed.

Gist:

- The notice is mostly telling the Guild to ____.

Evidence:

- I know this because the text says ____.

Context clue:

- I think ____ means ____ because nearby it says ____.

Reflection:

- The strategy that helped me was ____ because ____.

## Teacher notes

This quest should feel like the first real use of the system, not a test.

Do not overbuild lore. The text is only a frame for practising reading strategies.

Do not let party work replace solo evidence.

Do not require public oral reading.

## Completion rule

The quest is complete when the student submits solo evidence for the chosen route and logs one reflection or next move.

Completion does not require perfect reading. It requires clear evidence of strategy use.

## Follow-up

After this quest, the Game Master should check:

- Which path did each student choose?
- Did anyone avoid a path they may need soon?
- Which students need a reroute?
- Which students are ready for a first ability unlock?
- Is the class ready for more quest choice, or should choice stay narrow?
