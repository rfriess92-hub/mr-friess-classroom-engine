# Guild Engine Assessment and Privacy

## Purpose

Assessment information guides instruction and goal-setting. It is not a public game score.

## Private information

Keep individual progress check results, teacher notes, and student-specific reading information private.

## Public information

Public game elements may include quests, class milestones, party goals, completed strategy badges, and general campaign progress.

## Feedback pattern

Feedback should be short and useful:

1. What I noticed.
2. What it shows.
3. What to try next.

## Main rule

A game level is not an academic level. Character growth represents training, evidence, reflection, and improvement.

## Teacher control

The teacher decides what evidence counts, when students are ready to rank up, and how progress information is shared.
